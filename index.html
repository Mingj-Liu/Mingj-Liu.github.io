About

Mingju Liu is currently a PhD student in Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, supervised by Prof. Cunxi Yu. His research focuses on the hardware-software co-design of deep learning algorithms for addressing challenges in Electronic Design Automation (EDA). His research projects span across logic synthesis, high-level synthesis, and combinatorial optimization.

Mingju received his M.S. degree from Rutgers University in 2021 and his B.S. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2020. He was selected for the DAC Young Fellows Program in 2023 and received the ICCAD Student Scholar Travel Grant in 2024.
